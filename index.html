About Me

As a lifelong learner, I am both proud and fortunate enough to be involved in the field of education for the last several years. I have earned my undergraduate degree in teaching from The Pennsylvania State University and even grew up on my Grandfather's farm not too far from the University. Upon graduating, I moved to Fairfax, VA, where I was an instructor for 4 years and earned my masters degree in instructional technology. I have taught in New Jersey for 2 years after that, before moving into project management. I learned a vast amount of business skills while working as a project manager in the NYC area for a year and a half. All of my past experiences have brought me to where I am today as a web developer.
